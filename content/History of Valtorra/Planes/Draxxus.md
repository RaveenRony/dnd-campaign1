*The Maw Below*<br>
*Consumption, corruption, and endless hunger.*

![[static/images/draxxus.png|400]] <br>
Draxxus is a rotting labyrinth of fleshy tunnels, rusted chains, and magma rivers. It is said to be the devouring mouth of a dead god, twisted into a plane by infernal forces. Pain is currency, and betrayal is power.

**Inhabitants**:
 - Dark Lords - Ancient infernal lords who consume both flesh and memory. Exiled deities associated with darker domains or extremes
 - Hellborn Mawfiends - Demons with gaping maws and skeletal wings.
 - Flesh Scribes - Tortured souls that transcribe contracts into flesh using living ink. Appear in the mortal plane by possessing mortals to form contracts with mortals.

**Dark Lords**
1. The Devourer - God of Nature's Fury and Destruction
	- Dominion - Storms, earthquakes, fire, and primal wrath.
2. The Fury - Goddess of Passion and Madness
	- Dominion - Strong emotion, grief, vengeance, and obsession. 
3. The Keeper - God of Death and Greed
	- Dominion - Death, souls, greed, and the hoarding of power.
4. The Mockery - God of Treachery and Violence
	- Dominion - Betrayal, torture, dishonorable warfare.
5. The Shadow - God of Forbidden Magic and Ambition
	- Dominion - Corruption, shadow magic, and arcane secrets.
6. The Traveler -  God of Chaos and Change
	- Dominion - Change, deception, artifice, and transformation.
7. The Wither - Goddess of Decay and Stillness
	- Dominion -  Entropy, decay, disease, and the stillness between life and death.
8. The Hollow - God of Nihilism and War Without Purpose 
	- Dominion - Senseless violence, nihilism, bloodshed for its own sake.
