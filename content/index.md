**A world once whole, now broken — and on the edge of unraveling again.**

A millennium ago, the continent of **Elaria** shattered in a cataclysm known only as **The Shattering** — a world-splitting event that rained fire from the skies and tore the land into seven scattered isles. Now, the **Shattered Isles** stand as a fragmented remnant of a lost age, each island scarred by time, mystery, and creeping corruption.

This is the living archive of your journey across Valtorra. Here you'll find everything you need to navigate the chaos:
- **Map** of Elaria that show the frigid wastes of **Frostholm** to the wind-scoured sands of **The Forgotten Desert**
- **Player records** and prominent **NPC** profiles who shape the fate of Elaria
- **Factions, myths, artifacts**, and the whispers of gods long thought dead.
- **Session logs** and lore entries chronicling the unfolding tale.
But beware: knowledge is power — and some truths were hidden for a reason.

This is the wiki of the world that the campaign 1 - **The Shattering** is taking place in.
