*The Verdant Coil*
*Wild growth, primal spirits, and cyclical life.*

Thal’Zirra is an untamed, overgrown world where titanic trees pierce through floating islands, and vines strangle stone and sky alike. Every creature here is locked in a raw and unending struggle for survival and rebirth.

**Inhabitants**:
- Coilborn - Beast-spirit hybrids who serve as guardians of ancient natural cycles, often referred to as the last remaining Old Gods.
- Verdant Wyrms - Serpentine creatures that are born from primal ley lines.
- Grove-Minds - Hive-like consciousnesses made of moss and roots that offer wisdom

**Notable Feature**:
- **The Heartroot Cradle**: Said to be where all natural souls are born before being sent into the material realm. Also a powerful place of resurrection and druidic magic.
