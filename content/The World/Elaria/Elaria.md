Elaria, a place of breathtaking beauty and boundless life, was home to countless species, living in harmony — until the day that the same thunderous roar that was heard a millenia ago took place in Elaria. On that fateful day, a cataclysmic event unlike any before or since struck Elaria. The skies wept fire, the earth screamed as it tore itself apart, and in the span of a heartbeat, the great continent shattered into seven broken isles. This calamity came to be known as 'The Shattering.'

In the wake of such destruction, those who survived did so with scars that ran deeper than the wounds on their bodies. They rebuilt their homes and their lives on these fractured isles, but the memory of that day lingered like a dark cloud on the horizon, never truly gone, never truly understood. The true cause of The Shattering has remained a mystery, lost to time, whispered only as the wrath of the pantheon of the Dark Lords.

Now, centuries later, as life had become stable and nations were rebuilt, strange and foreboding phenomena have begun to stir on these shattered lands. Islands once teeming with life are now twisted by forces unknown, their inhabitants driven to the brink of madness, or worse — forced to flee. Already losing three out of the four islands, the people of Elaria had become more and more cautious to strange phenomena that takes place. The Shattered Isles, as Elaria is now known, stand on the precipice of another disaster, their people haunted by the echoes of a cataclysm that nearly ended them once before.
