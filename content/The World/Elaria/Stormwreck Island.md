*"The Eye of the Storm"*
*Isolation, Draconic Communion, Tempestuous Magic*

Endlessly surrounded by a magical hurricane, **Stormwreck Isle** is spoken of in hushed tones. Almost no vessels return once they enter the wall of stormclouds. But legends speak of ancient spires, ruined cities, and treasure hoards ruled by **Blue Dragons**, who have reshaped the island to suit their chaotic, storm-split domain. The entire isle is warped by elemental air and lightning. The clouds speak. The wind carries whispers. Magic itself feels wild and dangerous here. Little to nothing is known about this Isle.

<h4>Zephyr Crater</h4>

The rumored nesting ground of the eldest dragon, **Vaelzoryth the Thunderheart**, said to have seized the elemental core of the island. Ringed crackling ley lines, the crater is believed to hold a fragment of immense power, which gives Vaelzoryth her immense power.
