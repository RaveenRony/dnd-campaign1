*The Dreamfold*<br>
*Dreams, possibility, and forgotten truths.*

![[static/images/numinas.png|500]] <br>
Numinas is the shared dream of all sentient beings — a realm that constantly shifts based on the fears, hopes, and imaginations of the living. Time has no meaning here, and logic folds like paper.

**Inhabitants**:
 - Whisperlings - Fragile, moth-like spirits that collect dreams and nightmares of all beings.
 - The Sleepless Oracles - Beings that dwell forever in their dream, abandoning everything they are.
 - Shroudkin - Entities formed from collective nightmares, that slip into reality through unstable minds.
 
**Notable Feature**:
- **The Loom of Echoes**: A massive, translucent loom where the past, present, and future are woven together by unseen hands. Rare individuals can attempt to alter their destiny here — at a cost.
