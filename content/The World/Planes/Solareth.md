*The Luminous Sea on which the Sun never sets.*<br>
*Harmony, memory, and divine judgment.*

Solareth is a plane bathed in endless light that ripples like ocean waves. The land is made of glasslike crystal reefs that float in the radiant waters. Souls who lived lives of balance and honour sometimes ascend to this plane as "Lumen" — beings of light and wisdom.

**Inhabitants**:
- Celestials - Gods that rule over each dominion of power. Each Celestial provide balance to each Dark Lord stopping the balance of the World to fall.
- Vigil Seraphs - Celestial sentinels who watch the edges of the plane to prevent infernal corruption.
- Lumen - Beings of pure starlight, souls of those who lived with balance and honour   

**Celestials**
1. Aureon - God of Knowledge, Law, and Magic
	- Dominion - Lore, order, arcane power, and divine justice.
2. Balinor - God of the Hunt, Beasts, and Nature's Wrath
	- Dominion - The natural world, hunting, and survival.
3. Boldrei - Goddess of Community and Hearth
	- Dominion - Family, home, community, and safety.
4. Dol Arrah - Goddess of Honor, Sun, and Sacrifice
	- Dominion - Justice, martial honor, sunlight, and righteous war.
5. Dol Dorn - God of Strength and War
	- Dominion - Physical prowess, valor in battle, and sports.
6. Kol Korran - God of Trade, Wealth, and Commerce
	- Dominion - Markets, merchants, negotiation, and prosperity.
7. Olladra - Goddess of Luck and Feast
	- Dominion - Fortune, celebration, revelry, and the unexpected.
8. Onatar - God of Artifice, Forge, and Fire
	- Dominion - Creation, crafting, smithing, and invention.
